Common Questions

Do I need to buy expensive Montessori materials?
Not at all! That's the beauty of Monties Minders. Every activity uses items you already have at home - things like kitchen utensils, buttons, bowls, fabric scraps, and natural objects. We designed this workbook specifically to eliminate the barrier of expensive specialty materials. If we suggest something you don't have, we always provide common household alternatives.
How much time do I need for each activity?
Setup takes less than 15 minutes per activity, and many take just 5 minutes to prepare. Your child can then engage with the activity for 10-30 minutes depending on their interest and age. We understand you're busy - these activities fit into real life, not Pinterest-perfect fantasy schedules. You can do one activity per day, or several throughout the week.
I have no Montessori training. Will I be able to do this?
Absolutely yes! We created this workbook specifically for parents without formal training. Each activity includes step-by-step instructions, photos or illustrations, and explanations of what developmental skills your child is building. You'll learn the Montessori principles naturally as you go, without needing to take courses or read dense educational theory. If you can follow a recipe, you can use this workbook.
What if my child isn't interested in an activity?
That's completely normal and expected! Montessori is all about following the child's interests. The workbook includes 50+ activities specifically so you have variety and options. We provide guidance on how to observe your child's readiness and interest levels, and how to modify activities to match their developmental stage. Some activities your child will love immediately, others they'll come back to months later. There's no pressure to complete everything in order.
